Our Strategic Objectives

1. Promote awareness, access and retention to HIV and TB prevention and treatment services for adolescents, children and youth.
2. Advocate for promotion and protection of human rights and gender equality to increase access to HIV and TB prevention and treatment services for adolescents, children and youth
3. Advocate for increased domestic resources for health to ensure availability of better-quality HIV and TB prevention and treatment services.
4. Institutional strengthening of Lean on Me Foundation to be able to provide evidence-based, better quality services.

About Lean on Me Foundation

Lean on Me Foundation was founded to promote access to maternal, newborn, child and adolescent health in relation to HIV and Tuberculosis. Based in Kenya, we were registered through the Non-Government Coordination Board of Kenya in 2012. Lean on Me Foundation leads efforts towards building a strong movement against HIV and TB that mobilizes, advocates, mentors and raises consciousness on the issues that inform access and accelerate the end of the two diseases as epidemics. We aim to contribute to the achievement of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and are part of the broader movement for health and rights, working together in building on Universal Health Coverage (UHC2030) working in alliances with like-minded networks and organizations.
